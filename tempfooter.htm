About Us

This website is dedicated to Lama Tashi Dondup and The Kagyu Lineage. Our Aims are :
Encourage the study and practice of Buddhism.
Offer teachings and instructions on practices based on great masters of the Kagyu Lineage of Tibetan Buddhism.
Provide a place for taking refuge, and developing and strengthening our aspiration to live a meaningful life along the Buddhist path.
Support projects undertaken by masters of the Kagyu lineage worldwide.
Through this site, we hope to connect with all students of Lama Tashi worldwide and to anyone who is interested to learn more about Tibetan Buddhism.
